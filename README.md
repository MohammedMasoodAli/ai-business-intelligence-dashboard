# AI-Driven Business Intelligence & Decision Intelligence Platform

## Project Overview
This project is a web-based business intelligence platform designed for company executives and analysts.  
It transforms raw business data into interactive dashboards, AI-driven forecasts, and actionable insights.

## Problem Statement
Most businesses struggle to understand their data without complex tools.
This platform simplifies analytics by combining dashboards, forecasting, and natural-language AI insights.

## Key Features (Planned)
- Executive KPI Dashboard
- Revenue & Growth Analytics
- AI-based Forecasting (3–6 months)
- Natural Language "Ask Your Data" feature
- Automated Business Insights & Recommendations

## Target Users
- Business Owners
- Product Managers
- Business Analysts

## Tech Stack (Planned)
- Frontend: React + TypeScript
- Backend: Python (FastAPI)
- Database: PostgreSQL
- Analytics & AI: Pandas, Forecasting Models, LLMs
